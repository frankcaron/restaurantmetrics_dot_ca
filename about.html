the idea

Restaurant Metrics provides the tools and service you need to implement the right systems that ensure your restaurant's sustainability, longevity, and success.

In 2014, Restaurant Metrics was developed as unique cost control system that blends software and service to target and adjust specific areas and quickly to optimize profitability.

As we built out tools to help our clients succeed in their businesses, we chose to rebrand our entire offering to better represent the powerful combination of software and service and make it accessible to those that need it most.

Restaurant Metrics has now been implemented in over 50 restaurants across the city and has seen an average increase of 5% net profit, which for each restaurant translates to approximate $125,000 in annual savings.

Whether you are thinking of opening a new location or expanding your current one Food Forward can help lay the groundwork for your company's growth.



the history

We've been around the block,
and we've got some great stories to share.

Food Forward is a Toronto-based restaurant consulting company founded by Nanci Giovinazzo, a restaurant entrepreneur with over 25 years experience in the industry. Working with leading industry experts-architects, designers, builders, and chefs -to provide a full range of services from planning through building and operations through cost tracking and planning metrics.

Food Forward has provided consulting services to some of the city's best hospitality companies, and has been instrumental in successfully delivering value with a unique process that incorporates experience with innovation to deliver great results for every project we take on.
